Aside from N95s, it's also worth considering filtration through powered air purifiers for your home or office environment; even if you're fortunate enough to work from home and not deal wtih a commute or a toxic workplace, none of us are immune from climate change, whether through neverending (and ever-spreading) wildfires or run of the mill industrial pollution.
